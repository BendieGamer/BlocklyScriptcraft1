<h1>Online Classes</h1>
  Online classes are offered through Urbandale Community Education. Registration can be completed: <a href="https://www.urbandalecommunityed.com/programs/index">here</a><br>
  Classes are offered 3 times a week on Monday, Tuesday and Thursday<br>
  Each course consists of 12 30 minute classes over a 4 week period<br>
  Tuition is $150.<br>
<h2>Requirements</h2>
  Before registering students should possess: 
  <ul>
    <li>Windows 10 or MAC computer</li>
    <li>A MoJang minecraft account</li>
    <li>A microphone connected to their computer</li>
  </ul>
<h2>Notes</h2>
   Class time slots are 5:45, 6:30, 7:15, or 8:00, Monday, Tuesday and Thursday<br>
   2 days after the 4th week will be provided for makeup classes.<br>
