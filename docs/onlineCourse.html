Better Minecraft Games with Plugin Coding (Javascript)

This course will teach the fundamentals of programming, and use the minecraft computer game for testing
This 1-on-1 course consists of 12, 30 minute sessions with the instructor teaching the concept of: variables, if statements, while loops and function calls
Online classes are offered through Urbandale Community Education. Registration can be completed: here
Classes are offered 3 nights a week: Monday, Tuesday and Thursday
The time slot is chosen by the student and available at 4:30, 5:15, 6:00, 6:45, 7:30, and 8:15 PM
Class cost is $150.

Requirements

Before registering students should possess:
  • Windows 10 or MAC computer
  • A MoJang minecraft account
  • A microphone and speakers (or headset) connected to their computer

Notes

2 additional days are provided for makeup classes. These occur after the last week is complete.
Suggested age group: 8 to 16 years old
